Case Study · InternetGuard

Productionizing a NetGuard-based Android VPN security app

InternetGuard was a production Android VPN/security app based on NetGuard. My contribution focused on stabilizing production issues, improving VPN lifecycle behavior, handling Android compatibility, adding visibility through analytics/crash tracking, and improving the app experience around themes, localization, low-end devices, and promotion flows.

Challenge

Resolution

Focused on lifecycle/service stability and Android compatibility work so the app could behave more predictably across devices and OS versions.

Benefit

Challenge

Resolution

Worked with crash tracking and analytics events to make failures and user behavior easier to identify after release.

Benefit

Made quality improvements less reactive and gave the team clearer signals for what to fix next.

This case study is intentionally scoped: I am not claiming InternetGuard was built from scratch. The value of this work was production maintenance and improvement in a demanding Android security context where background behavior, OS compatibility, crashes, localization, and device performance directly affect the user's trust.

Additionally, I drove user acquisition by implementing an in-app cross-promotion campaign inside this VPN application to cross-promote our suite of security products, helping drive downloads for our apps from a baseline of 200 to around 10,000 and steadily increasing.
